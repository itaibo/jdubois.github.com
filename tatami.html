Tatami

Tatami is an Open Source Enterprise Social Network

I am the creator of Tatami, and I have been its lead developer for 2 years. I have stepped out of this role, as JHipster now takes all of my free time, but the project is still very active.

You can find a lot of information about Tatami on our Github page, and you can start using Tatami right now on our public SaaS at https://tatami.ippon.fr.
